How to measure

  • Chest is measured around the fullest part of your chest under the arms.
  • Length is from the high point of the shoulder to the bottom hem.
  • Sleeve length is from the shoulder seam to the end of the sleeve.
  • Most Rebel Reaper tops are cut true to size. If you're between sizes, size up for layering.
